Wind Speed

There is no direct relationship between wind speed and latitude. The maximum wind speed in our estimate was 33.4(mph) and was observed at a latitude of about -20. In general, wind speed did not exceed 15(mph) in most cities. Wind speeds above 25(mph) were observed in only 8 of all cities in the latitude range from about -35 to about 62.
